# Feedback and Suggestions

## Overall Experience

### 1. Working Environment

The working environment was professional, supportive, and collaborative. I received timely guidance that helped maintain stable project progress.

### 2. Mentor and Admin Support

Mentor support was practical and implementation-focused, especially for serverless architecture decisions and production-like troubleshooting.

### 3. Relevance to My Major

The internship tasks were highly aligned with my AI background while also expanding my cloud engineering and deployment knowledge.

### 4. Learning and Growth Opportunities

The program provided an end-to-end learning path from architecture design to deployment, testing, and documentation.

## Most Satisfying Part

- Building a complete technical workflow with real-world use cases.
- Solving real implementation issues (payload limits, timeouts, trigger setup).
- Receiving fast and useful mentor feedback.

## Suggested Improvements

- More deep-dive sessions on production best practices for AI inference workloads.
- Additional internal sessions on monitoring, tracing, and alerting.

## Future Expectation

I hope to continue with advanced topics such as cost optimization, security hardening, and real-time result delivery.

## Recommendation

I would recommend this internship program to students interested in Cloud and AI because it offers clear guidance, practical tasks, and strong learning value.
